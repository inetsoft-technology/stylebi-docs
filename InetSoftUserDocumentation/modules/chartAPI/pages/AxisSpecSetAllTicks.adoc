= AxisSpec.setAllTicks(Boolean)
:navtitle: setAllTicks()
%experimental%
include::ROOT:partial$buttonSnippets.adoc[]

Specifies whether *tick marks* are displayed for labels that have been suppressed by  xref:AxisSpecSetAbbreviate.adoc[] or because of duplicate-label suppression.  {noGetter}

== Parameter
Boolean:: `true`: show ticks for abbreviated (suppressed) labels +
`false`: hide ticks for abbreviated (suppressed) labels

<1> {dataset} Twelve monthly data points ensure enough labels to trigger abbreviation.
<2> Create the `AxisSpec` object. `setAbbreviate(true)` suppresses crowded labels; `setAllTicks(true)` keeps tick marks at all positions including suppressed ones.
<3> Use `setAllTicks(true)` to show tick marks at every month, even where the label has been suppressed.
