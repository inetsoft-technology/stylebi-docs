= AxisSpec.setTruncate(Boolean)
:navtitle: setTruncate()
%experimental%
include::ROOT:partial$buttonSnippets.adoc[]

Specifies whether axis labels are truncated with [`..`] when there is insufficient space to display the full text. When `false`, crowded labels may instead be hidden, overlapped, or rotated. {noGetter}

== Parameter
Boolean:: `true`: truncate labels that do not fit (default) +
`false`: do not truncate labels (crowded labels may be hidden or rotated)
