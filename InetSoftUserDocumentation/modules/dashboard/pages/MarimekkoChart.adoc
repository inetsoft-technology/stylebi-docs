Summary: Reference for creating a Marimekko (Mekko/mosaic) chart in Visual Composer, which displays a measure using variable-width and variable-height rectangles arranged in columns. Place a dimension in the X region for columns and another dimension in the Color or Break By region for the grouping within columns. MarimekkoChart encodes the same kind of hierarchical part-to-whole data as TreemapChart, SunburstChart, IcicleChart, and CirclePackingChart, but with a different binding: it is capped at a 2-level mosaic grid (X/Y/Color/Break By) rather than an arbitrary-depth hierarchy (T/Size/Color).
endif::ai[]

A Marimekko chart (also known as a Mekko or mosaic chart) displays a *measure* by using the sizes of rectangles in a hierarchical display.

include::ROOT:partial$measureAndDimensionTip.adoc[]

image::commonscript:MekkoConfig1.png[alt="marimekko chart example"]

To create a Marimekko chart, follow the basic steps below:

include::partial$chartNewbieTip.adoc[]

. Press the {buttonSelectChartStyle}. Choose the ‘Marimekko’ style. Press the {buttonApply}.
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfig2.png[alt="press Select Chart Style button"]

. From the ‘Dimensions’ folder of the *Data Source* panel, drag a desired *dimension* to the ‘X’ region. This dimension creates the X-axis column labels.
+
include::ROOT:partial$dimensionTip.adoc[]
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfigX.png[alt="drag dimension to X region"]

. From the ‘Measures’ folder of the *Data Source* panel, drag a desired *measure* to the ‘Y’ region.
+
include::ROOT:partial$measureTip.adoc[]
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfig3.png[alt="drag measure to Y region"]

. Drag the desired Mekko category *dimension* from the *Data Source* panel to the ‘Color’ region. This dimension determines the grouping within each column of the chart.
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfigColor.png[alt="drag Mekko category dimension to Color region"]

. *_Optional:_* Drag the same Mekko category dimension to the ‘Text’ region to label each segment.

. *_Optional:_* Press the ‘Edit Color’ button next to the Mekko category dimension, and choose the desired colors to represent its values. Press the {buttonApply}.
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfig4.png[alt="press Edit Color button"]

. *_Optional:_* Drag a dimension from the *Data Source* panel to the ‘Break By’ region to break out the data further within each column.

. *_Optional:_* {multipleDimensionNoColor}

. {finishEditing}
+
image::commonscript:MekkoConfig1.png[alt="completed marimekko chart"]

NOTE: To create a grid of charts, place dimensions into the ‘X’ and/or ‘Y’ regions as well. See xref:TrellisChartGrid.adoc[] for more information.
