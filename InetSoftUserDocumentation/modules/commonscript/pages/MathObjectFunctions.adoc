The Math object provides the following numerical constants:

[cols="1,3"]
|===
|Constant|Description

|Math.E| Euler’s number, e ≈ 2.718281828459045
|Math.LN10| The natural log of 10, ln10 ≈ 2.302585092994046
|Math.LN2| The natural log of 2, ln2 ≈ 0.6931471805599453
|Math.LOG10E| The base 10 log of e, log10e  ≈ 0.4342944819032518
|Math.LOG2E|  The base 2 log of e, log2e  ≈ 1.4426950408889634
|Math.PI| The value π ≈ 3.141592653589793. See also xref:commonscript:CALC_pi.adoc[].
|Math.SQRT1_2| The square root of 1/2 ≈ 0.7071067811865476
|Math.SQRT2| The square root of 2 ≈ 1.4142135623730951
|===
